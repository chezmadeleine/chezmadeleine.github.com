Welcome to Chez Madeleine Bed & Breakfast. Located on the borders of Lauzun, 10 minutes from Eymet and 30 minutes from Bergerac Airport, we are ideally situated for your visit to the area, its chateaux, vineyards, medieval towns and villages. Ryanair and Fly Be have regular flights to Bergerac Airport. Our family suite, with its own luxurious bathroom, can sleep up to four. We also have a large, comfortable twin bedded guest room with generous en suite facilities. Rooms are provided with an alarm clock and hair dryer. There is an attractive balcony to catch the sun as well as a guest lounge, small garden and external barbeque. Parking facilities are available and there is also secure parking for motorcyles and bikes to the rear. Guests have their own entrance. We offer continental breakfast and can provide a gluten free breakfast if advised in advance. The sunny balcony is the perfect place to sip a glass of wine and relax after a long day sightseeing. For a nominal fee, you can use the private kitchen to prepare your evening meal. The kitchen is fully equipped with a gas hob, microwave, kettle and steam iron. If you fancy a night out, both Eymet and Castillones offer a wide selection of restaurants and bars and there is a pleasant bar and restaurant nearby at Lauzun.
